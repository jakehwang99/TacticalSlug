Office Hours

Office hours are 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Friday, excluding major holidays. The office is closed from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m. for lunch.

Schedules

Our school is designed for full time students (Monday - Friday). In an effort to accommodate the community needs, there are limited part time schedules available. A three day schedule means Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Please refer to the admission section for guidelines in placement.

Schedule Changes

All Schedule changes must be submitted at least 30 days in advance of the date you are requesting your child's schedule to change. Forms are available in the preschool office and must be filled out with the necessary information. All schedule changes are subject to a $30.00 administration fee.
